How It Works

1. ML Signal Generation

Trained an XGBoost classifier on 50+ engineered features including trend indicators, momentum oscillators, volatility measures, and market regime characteristics. The model predicts trade direction with 68% accuracy on unseen data.

2. Risk Management

Implemented dynamic position sizing based on volatility-adjusted Kelly Criterion. Position size automatically scales down during high volatility periods and increases during stable conditions. Stop losses set at 2x ATR, take profits at 3x ATR for consistent 1.5:1 risk/reward.

3. MT5 Automation

The Expert Advisor reads ML predictions from a CSV file updated by Python every hour, executes trades automatically, manages open positions with trailing stops, and logs all activity for performance monitoring.

Validation Process:

  • Walk-forward analysis with rolling 2-year training windows
  • Monte Carlo simulation (1,000 iterations) for risk assessment
  • Transaction cost modeling including spread, slippage, and commissions
  • Performance tested across bull, bear, and ranging markets

Technology & Key Learnings

Tech Stack:

Python 3.9+ XGBoost scikit-learn pandas & numpy MQL5 MetaTrader 5 Backtrader

What Worked:

  • Feature selection: Top 20 features outperformed using all 50+
  • Regime awareness: Separate logic for trending vs ranging markets improved performance by 30%
  • Simple models: XGBoost with shallow trees generalized better than deep neural networks
  • Dynamic sizing: Volatility adjustment reduced drawdown significantly

What Didn't Work:

  • Complex LSTM networks overfitted badly on financial time series data
  • High-frequency approaches killed profitability due to transaction costs
  • Pure technical signals without ML filtering resulted in 45% win rate
